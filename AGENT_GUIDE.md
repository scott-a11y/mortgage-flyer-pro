## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How often do rates update?
**A:** Rates update in real-time when clients view the flyer. The system fetches the latest rates each time the page loads.

### Q: Can I customize my flyer?
**A:** Your mortgage broker creates and customizes the flyer with your information. Contact them to update your photo, contact details, or branding.

### Q: Does the link expire?
**A:** No! Your flyer link stays active indefinitely. Share it once and clients can return anytime for current rates.

### Q: Can clients apply for a mortgage from the flyer?
**A:** The flyer includes a QR code and call-to-action button that links to the mortgage application. Clients can scan or click to start their application.

### Q: What if a client has questions about rates?
**A:** Your contact information is prominently displayed on the flyer. Clients can call, text, or email you directly.

### Q: Can I track who views my flyer?
**A:** Ask your mortgage broker about analytics options. Some setups include view tracking and engagement metrics.

### Q: Is this mobile-friendly?
**A:** Yes! The flyer is fully responsive and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers.

### Q: Can I share this on social media?
**A:** Absolutely! You can share the link directly or download pre-sized graphics optimized for each platform.
